TravMate


New age app for new age travellers

There is an app for any thing you could possibly imagine, but Travel is often overlooked and there is no good application that matches users expectations. TravMate(unofficial name) is the one that is going to make a change in this area.
Github Repository

What is it about?

With the aim of building an exceptionally brilliant application which can serve as the defacto app for all your travel needs, we bring you TravMate! TravMate is an application that helps you meet new people, share ideas, exchange interests and most importantly, it builds a community of travellers from all around the world.

It's intuitive

Intuitiveness is the core element of TravMate! We are bringing this app for all kinds of users and all kinds of travellers.

This app is your ultimate companion for your next travel adventure. Be it to an exotic country or a cinema theatre near you. This app will bridge the gap between all that's there and all that's not!

Features

Online Chat

Chat with people who have already travelled to your next favourite destination or just get to know about people who are planning the same trip.

Location based filtering

View users' profiles only near you or view everyone. Your choice!

Get to know people better

With rich user profile information, get to know your interested person better, before making a travel plan with them.
